## Cost

* Should cost get higher in Decorators?
* CostBalance:
    * For every 2 points in stats the cost multiplies per point by 1.5.
    * 1 = 1
    * 2 = 2 (* 1.5) = 3
    * 3 = 4
    * 4 = 4 (* 1.5) = 6
    * 5 = 7 
* Base cost for stats is 1 gold.
